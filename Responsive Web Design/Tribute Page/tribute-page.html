Tribute to Princess Daisy

The Mario universe's best princess.

Here's a few reasons why Princess Daisy is so great.

  • She's the princess of Sarasaland, a much more interesting and diverse land than the Mushroom Kingdom
  • She's got much more spunk and personality than the other two princesses. She'd never back down from a challenge.
  • She no longer battles with the others over Mario, taking more interest in Luigi instead.
